About Me

Hello! My name is Jason Yang and I am a Junior Computer Science student at the University of California, Los Angeles. I'm currently looking for an internship for the summer of 2016. I'm currently proficient at the C++/C languages but have some experience and am interested in front-end and mobile development. I had the opportunity to lead my own committee and help fundraise over $20,000 to various charities we support. I've also spent a lot a time singing with my A Cappella Group, AweChords. I was the treasurer last year and now act as the group's president.
